24 Jain Tirthankaras and their symbol

In Jainism, a Tirthankara is a saviour and spiritual teacher of the dharma .The word tirthankara tells about the founder of a tirtha which is a fordable passage across the sea of interminable births and deaths. According to Jains, a Tirthankara is an individual who has conquered the samsara, the cycle of death and rebirth, on their own, and made a path for others to follow . Tirthankara is also called as Jina and Arihantas
